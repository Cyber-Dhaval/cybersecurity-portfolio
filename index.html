VAPT Methodology

1. Scoping & Rules of Engagement

Define scope, assets, and constraints. Confirm authorization and objectives.

2. Recon & Threat Modeling

Map assets, trust boundaries and threats (STRIDE); build DFDs and attack paths.

3. Vulnerability Discovery

Manual & automated testing (OWASP, misconfigs, auth/session, input handling).

4. Exploitation & Validation

Validate impact safely in controlled lab or with explicit consent.

5. Reporting & Remediation

Risk rating, evidence, reproducible steps, prioritized fixes & retest plan.

6. Detection & Hardening

Map to MITRE ATT&CK, add SIEM detections, logging, and security controls.
